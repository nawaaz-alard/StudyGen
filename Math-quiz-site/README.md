# Math Explorer - Grade 8 Study Hub

Welcome to the **Vibrant Grade 8 Math Hub**, a gamified progressive web application (PWA) designed to make mastering mathematics engaging for students while providing visibility for parents and teachers.

## ğŸ“ Features

### For Students
- **Gamified Learning**: Earn **Math-Points (XP)** for every correct answer. Level up from "Beginner" to "Math Wizard".
- **Math Shop**: Use your XP to unlock visual themes (like *Vintage Paper* or *Neon Night*) and badges.
- **Speed Blitz**: A fast-paced minigame to practice terminology (Algebra, Geometry, Numbers) text-rain style.
- **Focus Mode**: A built-in **Pomodoro Timer** (25min) and **Dark Mode** to reduce eye strain during late-night study.
- **Working Area**: A digital canvas (`Tools` -> `Working Area`) to scribble calculations directly on the screen.

### For Parents & Teachers
- **Progress Reports**: Instantly generate a **downloadable text report** (`Tools` -> `Report Card`) showing:
  - Current Level and XP.
  - Number of pending study goals.
  - Mastery status of topics.
- **Standard Aligned**: Content covers **Paper 1** (Algebra, Integers) and **Paper 2** (Geometry, Measurement) based on Grade 8 curriculum.
- **Offline Capable**: The app works fully offline once loaded, safe for tablets/phones without constant internet.
